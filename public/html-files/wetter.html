About Fürth

Location & Topography

Fürth is located at 49.476° latitude, 10.989° longitude, at an elevation of about 302 meters (991 ft) in Bavaria, Germany.

The topography within 2 miles contains only modest variations in elevation, with a maximum elevation change of 89 meters (292 feet).

The area within 2 miles of Fürth is covered by artificial surfaces (74%) and cropland (14%), within 10 miles by cropland (36%) and trees (31%).

The city is situated in the Middle Franconia region and is closely connected to the neighboring city of Nuremberg.
